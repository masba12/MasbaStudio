A lanyard card holder is a practical accessory designed to hold and display identification cards, badges, or other essential cards during events, conferences, or everyday use. 

It typically consists of a clear plastic pouch or sleeve attached to a lanyard, allowing individuals to wear their identification cards conveniently around their necks.

The card holder can be customized with various designs, logos, or additional features to suit specific branding or functional requirements.

Vertical: 7cm (W) X 10cm (H)
